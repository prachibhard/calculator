Methodology

The calculation is based on the prices of products sold in CVS, Walgreens, and Rite Aid pharmacies located in Times Square, New York. The products were chosen by identifying brands that had both male- and female-marketed products, or separate male- and female-marketed brands with a similarly tiered price point (mid-tier) and similar volumes (in ounces). For the purpose of this calculation, any 2-in-1 shampoo and conditioner product will be considered a “male-marketed” item. The only store-brand item will be the daily vitamins. [See Data]
